Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age requirement?

We offer classes for students aged 5 and up. Different programs are available for children, teens, and adults to ensure age-appropriate training.

Do I need previous experience?

No prior experience is required. Our beginner programs are designed to help you start from the basics and progress at your own pace.

What should I bring to class?

For your first class, comfortable workout clothes and water. Once enrolled, we'll provide guidance on required uniforms and equipment.

Can I switch between different styles?

Core and Elite members can train in multiple styles. We encourage cross-training to develop well-rounded martial arts skills.

Is there a contract commitment?

We offer both month-to-month and annual membership options. Annual memberships come with additional savings.

What is your cancellation policy?

Monthly memberships require 30 days notice for cancellation. Annual memberships can be transferred or frozen if needed.
